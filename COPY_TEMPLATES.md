## HOW IT WORKS - PROCESS COPY

### Step 1: Consultation
```
We start with a conversation. Share your vision, inspiration, and ideas. 
This is where we understand your style, your story, and what you're looking 
for. No pressure—just creative collaboration.

Typically: 15-30 minutes | Free | In-person or via Zoom
```

### Step 2: Design & Approval
```
Based on our consultation, a custom design is created specifically for 
you—taking into account your body, the placement, and your aesthetic 
preferences. You'll review drafts and request revisions until it's perfect.

Typically: 2-5 days | Included in service | Digital mockups provided
```

### Step 3: Preparation
```
Once your design is approved, we schedule your tattoo session and prepare 
you with everything you need to know: what to wear, what to bring, how to 
prepare your skin, and what to expect during the session.

Typically: 1 week before appointment | Email guide provided
```

### Step 4: The Session
```
This is where vision becomes reality. You'll be in a clean, comfortable, 
professional environment. We go at a pace that works for you, explaining 
each step and ensuring you're comfortable throughout.

Typically: 1-4 hours depending on size & complexity
```

### Step 5: Aftercare & Follow-Up
```
A detailed aftercare guide ensures your tattoo heals perfectly and stays 
vibrant for life. Follow-up check-ins at key healing stages. Touch-ups 
included if needed.

Typically: 2-4 weeks healing | Ongoing support
```

## FAQ SECTION COPY

### Q: What styles do you specialize in?
**A:**
```
I primarily focus on fine line designs, custom work, minimalist pieces, 
and geometric patterns. Every design is custom—I don't do flash tattoos. 
If you have an idea or inspiration, we can bring it to life in a style 
that suits your vision and your skin.
```

### Q: What does a tattoo cost?
**A:**
```
Pricing depends on the size, complexity, and placement of your design. 
Fine line pieces start at $[amount]. Larger or more complex work typically 
ranges from $[amount] to $[amount]+.

All consultations are free—during that conversation, I can give you an 
accurate quote based on your specific vision.

A 50% deposit is required to secure your appointment date.
```

### Q: What if I don't have a design yet?
**A:**
```
No problem—that's actually very common. During our consultation, we'll 
discuss your style, inspiration, and ideas. From there, I'll create a 
custom design just for you. You'll be involved every step of the way, 
with revisions until it's perfect.
```

### Q: How long does a tattoo take?
**A:**
```
It depends on the size and complexity of your design:
- Small fine line piece: 30-60 minutes
- Medium design: 1-2 hours
- Larger work: 2-4 hours or multiple sessions

During your consultation, I'll give you an accurate time estimate.
```

### Q: What's the current wait time?
**A:**
```
Due to high demand and my commitment to custom work, the wait time is 
currently [X] weeks. This ensures each client gets the attention and 
quality they deserve.

You can check current availability and book a consultation here: [Link]
```

### Q: Do you accept walk-ins?
**A:**
```
No. All work is by appointment only. This allows me to give each client 
dedicated time for consultation and design work.

To book: [Link to booking system or contact info]
```

### Q: Do you use sterile equipment?
**A:**
```
Absolutely. I maintain the highest standards of sterilization and safety:
- New needles for every client
- Autoclaved equipment
- Single-use inks
- Professional-grade sanitation procedures
```

### Q: What's the aftercare process?
**A:**
```
You'll receive a detailed aftercare guide with specific instructions for 
your tattoo. Key points:
- Keep it clean and dry for the first 24 hours
- Apply [recommended ointment] for [X] days
- Avoid swimming and excessive sun for [X] weeks
- No picking or scratching during healing

I'm available for questions throughout the healing process.
```

### Q: Can I modify a design after consulting?
**A:**
```
Yes. The design phase is collaborative. If you want to adjust anything—
size, placement, elements—just let me know. Minor revisions are included. 
Major redesigns may have an additional fee.
```

### Q: Do you offer touch-ups?
**A:**
```
Yes. Touch-ups within [X] months of your original tattoo are included at 
no cost. After that, they're available at a discounted rate.

Most fine line tattoos age beautifully and rarely need touch-ups if proper 
aftercare is followed.
```

### Q: Can I bring inspiration photos?
**A:**
```
Absolutely. Bring any references, photos, or inspiration to your 
consultation. I'll use them to understand your style and create something 
uniquely yours—not a copy, but inspired by what you love.
```

### Q: What if I'm nervous?
**A:**
```
Totally understandable. Many first-time clients feel nervous, and that's 
completely normal. During your session, I'll:
- Explain each step before doing it
- Go at a comfortable pace
- Check in regularly on how you're feeling
- Take breaks if needed

Most clients find the experience more comfortable than they expected.
```

## NOTES ON CUSTOMIZATION

When using these templates:

1. **Replace [X] with actual numbers**
   - Years of experience
   - Number of clients
   - Current wait time
   - Cost ranges

2. **Replace [City] with your location**
   - Helps with local SEO
   - Makes copy more relevant
   - Builds local authority

3. **Replace [LINK] with actual links**
   - Booking links
   - Portfolio links
   - Contact forms
   - Social media

4. **Make it personal**
   - Add your voice/personality
   - Reference your specific specialties
   - Include unique details about your process
   - Share what makes you different

5. **Test different versions**
   - A/B test headlines
   - Try different CTAs
   - Measure what resonates
   - Optimize based on performance

6. **Keep it updated**
   - Update wait times monthly
   - Add new testimonials
   - Feature recent work
   - Reflect any process changes
